# Internationalization declaration

**I18N-Standard: Applies**

**i18n status: Applies — deferred to the next minor release, no later than 2026-10-24**

**Owner:** Project maintainer

**Current state:** English-only public site; no locale catalog exists

Reason: The source/evidence/analysis boundary is safety-critical, so the
first localized slice needs a reviewed catalog and source-language rules
instead of unreviewed direct translation.

**Review deadline:** before the next minor release, and no later than the
Section 1 planning date shown by the product (October 24, 2026)

The first localization slice covers navigation, search and filter labels,
empty and error states, the source/evidence/analysis boundary, correction
instructions, and the methodology summary. The signed executive order,
reviewed excerpts, organization names, evidence quotations, and citations
remain in their source language and are labelled as such; a translation must
never replace or blur the signed source.

Implementation requires stable message identifiers, locale-aware dates and
numbers, a documented fallback chain, and catalog parity/staleness checks.
Human review by a California transportation-language reviewer is required
before a locale is advertised as supported. Stale localized copy falls back
to clearly labelled English rather than being presented as current.

This declaration closes the ownership and decision-date gaps only. It does not
claim that translation research, catalog infrastructure, translated copy, or
review evidence already exists.
